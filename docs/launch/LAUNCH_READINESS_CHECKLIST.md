## Risk Management
## إدارة المخاطر

### Identified Risks

#### Technical Risks
| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|------------|--------|------------|
| Infrastructure overload | Medium | High | Pre-scaled, auto-scaling |
| Database bottleneck | Low | High | Read replicas, caching |
| CDN failure | Low | Medium | Multi-CDN setup |
| Security breach | Low | Critical | WAF, monitoring, audits |
| Third-party service down | Medium | Medium | Fallback providers |

#### Business Risks
| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|------------|--------|------------|
| Low user adoption | Medium | High | Marketing campaign ready |
| Negative feedback | Low | Medium | Rapid response team |
| Competitor action | Medium | Medium | Unique value prop |
| Content compliance issue | Low | Critical | Sharia board review |
| Support overwhelm | Medium | Medium | Over-staffed day 1 |
