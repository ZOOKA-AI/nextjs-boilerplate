# Sharia Compliance Guidelines
## إرشادات التوافق الشرعي

### Overview
**نظرة عامة**

This document outlines the Sharia compliance guidelines for the Zooka platform, ensuring that all features, content, and operations align with Islamic principles and values.

---

## Core Principles
## المبادئ الأساسية

### 1. Halal Content Only
**محتوى حلال فقط**

The platform must:
- ✅ Promote beneficial knowledge and skills
- ✅ Respect Islamic values and ethics
- ✅ Support family and community wellbeing
- ❌ Never include gambling or games of chance
- ❌ Never promote alcohol or intoxicants
- ❌ Never include inappropriate imagery or content
- ❌ Never facilitate prohibited (haram) activities

### 2. Privacy and Modesty
**الخصوصية والحشمة**

- User privacy is paramount (الخصوصية أمانة)
- Gender-appropriate interactions
- Respect for personal boundaries
- Secure data handling as a trust (أمانة)
- No unauthorized surveillance

### 3. Transparency and Honesty
**الشفافية والصدق**

- Clear terms of service
- Honest representation of features
- No hidden fees or deceptive practices
- Transparent data usage policies
- Fair and ethical business practices

### 4. Social Responsibility
**المسؤولية الاجتماعية**

- Benefit to the Ummah (community)
- Educational value
- Supporting good causes
- No harm to individuals or society
- Promoting unity and understanding

## Sharia Board Oversight
## إشراف المجلس الشرعي

### Board Composition
**تكوين المجلس**

The Sharia Advisory Board should include:
- Qualified Islamic scholars (علماء)
- Technology experts with Islamic knowledge
- Legal professionals (فقهاء)
- Community representatives
- Ethics specialists

### Board Responsibilities
**مسؤوليات المجلس**

- [ ] Review platform features before launch
- [ ] Ongoing compliance monitoring
- [ ] Issue resolution and fatwa when needed
- [ ] Policy updates and recommendations
- [ ] Quarterly compliance reports
- [ ] User complaint review
- [ ] Annual comprehensive audit

### Review Process
**عملية المراجعة**

1. **Initial Review**: All new features submitted to board
2. **Analysis**: Board reviews against Islamic principles
3. **Consultation**: Discussion and deliberation
4. **Decision**: Approval, modification, or rejection
5. **Documentation**: Reasoning documented
6. **Implementation**: Approved changes implemented
7. **Follow-up**: Ongoing monitoring

## Incident Response
## الاستجابة للحوادث

### Compliance Violations
**انتهاكات الامتثال**

#### When Prohibited Content is Found:
1. **Immediate Removal**: Content removed within 1 hour
2. **User Notification**: Inform user of violation
3. **Documentation**: Record incident details
4. **Analysis**: Understand how it bypassed filters
5. **Prevention**: Update systems to prevent recurrence
6. **Board Notification**: Inform Sharia board of significant issues

#### Escalation Levels:
- **Level 1**: Minor violations → Automated removal
- **Level 2**: Moderate violations → Human review + warning
- **Level 3**: Serious violations → Account suspension + board review
- **Level 4**: Severe violations → Permanent ban + legal action if needed
